Course of Study Alignment Quiz

Below you will find a short and simple quiz that you can use as a tool to help you decide what path of study you would like to take in the years ahead of you.

It can be challenging for some to decide by themselves what the next three years of school will be like, especially if you were given limited information in the first place as to the specifics of the two courses.

This quiz should not decide for you, but instead give you an idea of where your strengths lie and what course your character type is typically more alligned with.
